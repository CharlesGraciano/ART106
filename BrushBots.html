Wearable:

  • For my wearable, I wanted to create a fun button to press. Something that was able to retract whenever it was being pressed and stays in its shape when it isn't. I used foam from an old rc car tire. I took the rubber off of the foam and cut the foam in half. I reattached the foam in the opposite direction, to give the negative space of the button a little resistance. I bonded both sides of the button with staples and electrical tape. It is very saturday and attached to the 9 volt battery, gives the lights strength and luminosity that is desired.
